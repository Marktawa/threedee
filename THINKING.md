The idea is that I will make a Facebook page for Three Dee Day Care School located in Chingola.

Page set up includes putting all the necessary details in play. So we got the name of the school, where it is located, it's working hours, what it offers academically, what it offers extra-curricularly, what makes it stand out, why parents should consider, the future plans for the school, contact details for the staff, the fees, payment options available.

For weekly posts, we will talk about the highlights of the week in detail. This is a simple overview of what was happening at the school that is worth sharing with the public. It could be an event, the commencement of school, academic high achievers, planning for some activity, sports activities. The key thing to take note of is the description, so teachers can highlight what was of note in their experience during the week. Sometimes such reflections can lead to more interesting engagements.
