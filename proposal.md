## Job Role Overview
A Facebook Page Manager is responsible for managing the Facebook page of a business. For Three Dee Day Care and Primary School, this means using the Facebook page as a tool to promote the school, share information and updates and turn visitor interest into real leads for the school business.

## Why Am I Applying for the Role
I noticed that your school has a Facebook page so thought of offering my services. A well-managed Facebook page for your school. A Facebook page that makes it easier for parents to find you online, highlights what makes your school unique, and keeps your community informed all in one place.

My goal is to help your school stay active, visible, and professional online without you having to worry about managing the Facebook page. That way you can manage other important issues like day to day tasks at the school.

My role is to complement your efforts on the ground so that the school can have a steady flow of interest from people who may want to bring their children to the school.

## Job Role In Detail
The central goal of this role is to turn the Three Dee Day Care and Primary School facebook page into a channel for finding parents who want to enrol their children at the school. Everything I do will work towards that goal. Here is how the work flows in practice.

### The Foundation: A Properly Built Facebook Page
Before anything else, the school needs a properly built Facebook page. A properly built Facebook page has the following:
- A clear name of the school to identify it
- A clear and welcoming description of the school in the description section of the page
- Information about when it was established and its vision found in the "About Us" section
- A list of activites and services offered (early primary education, playing, napping, meals, transport, events and so on)
- Location information, Contact numbers and working hours
- A short section on why parents should choose your school
- Frequently asked questions and answers
- A nice cover picture and profile picture that represent the school well.
- An introductoory post that welcomes visitors when they see your page.

All these details help make the school's Facebook page look professional and help reassure parents who are looking for a school for their children.
